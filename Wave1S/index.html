ABOUT THE RSM CLASSIC

A Seaside
Tradition

For years, The RSM Classic has been a Seaside Tradition for golfers, fans and the RSM family alike. Located on the southern tip of St. Simons Island, the Sea Island Golf Club boasts unparalleled courses, providing an unmatched setting to host the final event on the PGA TOUR’s schedule.

We are excited to welcome you to the ultimate client experience to take part in all that it has to offer. It’s this tradition of deep understanding that fuels our passion for supporting companies like yours, as well as the community, to not only see what’s possible, but soar beyond it.

Stewardship

Showing Up for Others

For over 90 years, RSM has been driven in their mission to give back by supporting local communities, with over $36 million dollars donated to organizations benefitting children and families. The RSM Classic, hosted by Davis Love III and the Davis Love Foundation, shares our vision of what it means to give back. The Power of Love campaign, as RSM’s annual stewardship program, engages employees and friends of the firm to give back in their local communities, and is supported by a generous match from the RSM US Foundation. See what a difference the Power of Love made in 2022.

Accommodations

A stay that’s a step above

With five miles of private beach, incredible dining and unmatched Southern hospitality, a stay at Sea Island Resort ensures your visit is as unforgettable as The RSM Classic itself. As part of your invitation and accommodations at this luxury Forbes Five-Star resort, you’ll have the opportunity to choose between optional activities including golf, yoga and clay target shooting, while enjoying the beauty the resort has to offer.
